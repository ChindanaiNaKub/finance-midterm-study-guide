üíµ INCOME & INFLATION

Nominal Income Change:

Nominal change % = (New income - Old income) / Old income √ó 100

Ex: Old:$37K, New:$38.6K ‚Üí Nominal=4.3%

Real Income Change:

Real change % = Nominal change % - Inflation rate %

Ex: Nominal:4.3%, Inflation:4.0% ‚Üí Real=0.3%

Real Income in Dollars:

Real income = Nominal income √ó (1 + real change as decimal)

Ex: $37K √ó (1+0.003) = $37,111
